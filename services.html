Market Research/Due Diligence

Impact Consult has a very strong business development department that attends to all business intelligence, market research and due diligence needs of our clients. Whether it is the introduction of new products or assessing the latent market potentials of an existing product, ourn team rises up to the challenge and exceed your expectations and offer tailor-made research to make your brand competitive. We are also experienced in database management, automated charting and data delivery useful for large tracking studies or multi region research.

Baseline Survey & Monitoring

Impact Initiative is proficient at conducting baseline, mid-line and end-line surveys for all kinds of projects. Our clientele base span across government agencies, bilateral agencies, multilateral agencies and non-governmental agencies. Our M&E ensure that projects are on track and achieving its objectives for being. We firmly believe that a development activity entails change, so a good M&E and impact assessment will show whether the envisaged changes are being realized. Our reports help you assess whether the results are intended or not, direct or indirect, positive or negative, primary or secondary and suggest how to improve the efficiency of implementation, the extent of the desired results achieved and their sustainability.

Mystery Shopping &Customer Intelligence

Companies spend fortunes advertising to attract new customers to their business but fail to convert these customers into loyal customers who in turn generate several leads for the firm. Indeed, adverts are crucial in attracting new customers but failing to keep new customers is a needless waste of resources. Our team of researchers provides this advantage by giving you crucial feedback that enables you assess whether your customers are satisfied or not. We use trained observers who pose as customers or potential customers and are hence able to accurately identify the deficiencies in your service delivery processes at all points of contact with the customer and staff.

Feasibility Study

A feasibility study looks at the viability of an idea or decision with an emphasis on identifying potential problems and attempts to answer one main question “Will the idea or decision work and should I proceed with it”? Our team therefore provides in-depth details about the business to determine if and how it can succeed, and serve as a valuable tool for developing a winning business plan. From our work, you will be guided in your decisions as to the potential benefits or probable losses of taking up a prospective venture before undertaking the actual investing

Academic Mentoring & Research Writing

Impact Initiatives hold Thesis Clinics or Training Workshops when students express the need for such services. This comprises training in the dos and don’ts in the various stages of the thesis/long essay write-up. Our expert statisticians will train you on how to couch your research hypothesis, the appropriate statistical methods or tools to use in the analysis as well as the statistical softwares that will be appropriate in your data analysis. We also a software that digitizes your questionnaire and sends the completed questionnaire to a centralized database for analysis without going through the onerous data entry exercise.

Business Plan & Proposal Writing

A well written, straight-to-the-point but comprehensively strategic business plan is always your corporate operational guide; a tool to measure your progress; a compass in your business journey, and also a basis for evaluating the viability of your enterprise. We create a business plan that includes everything a banker, investor or governmental body may require to make an investment decision. Our business planning services include working with the client to prepare bespoke business plans that ensures all nuanced details of the business have been captured and adequately presented without compromising the simplicity and persuasive nature of the plan/proposal.

Real Estate Consultancy

The Real Estate department helps companies and individuals make better real estate decisions and get value for money in such undertakings. Through our extensive and diverse valuation and consulting experience, we have developed a thorough understanding of the interplay among asset valuation, capital markets and today’s changing real estate marketplace issues. The Real Estate Consulting team covers the full spectrum of real estate valuation services, ranging from Lease Abstraction, Lease Audit, Real Estate Due Diligence, Real Estate Highest and Best Use Studies, Real Estate Impact Studies and Real Estate Market Studies.
