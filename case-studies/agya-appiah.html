Agya Appiah Bitters Limited (AABL)

Agya Appiah Bitters Limited (AABL) has been in the alcoholic beverage production business for the past 33 years.

The company is noted for the production and distribution of herb-based alcoholic drinks in Ghana, with export of same products to neighboring African countries, particularly Nigeria, Liberia and Togo.

AABL, having been in the production and distribution of herb-based alcoholic drinks for over three decades, can rightfully be regarded as the market leader in the production of herb-based alcoholic beverages. The company contracted Impact Initiatives to put together its business case and lead the process towards securing funds to expand and consolidate its West African market share.
